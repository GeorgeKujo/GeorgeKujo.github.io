About

Independent research beyond institutional boundaries.

George Kujo works where medical ethics, public policy, consumer protection, and institutional accountability meet.

Biography

Research begins where institutions stop explaining themselves.

George Kujo is an independent researcher and public commentator examining how emergency measures become permanent systems, how administrative decisions affect individual rights, and how evidence can be translated into accountable public policy.

His work distinguishes academic publication from public commentary while connecting both to the same traceable evidence base.

Methods

Projects draw on public records, government guidance, secondary datasets, legal and policy documents, citation audit, and adversarial review. Methods and limitations are documented at project level.

Research identity

Research outputs are connected through ORCID and preserved through DOI-linked journal pages or open archives such as Zenodo.

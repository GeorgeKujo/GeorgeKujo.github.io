About

Research begins where institutions stop explaining themselves.

I am an independent researcher and writer examining institutional failures, overlooked rights, and the distance between evidence, policy, and lived experience.

My work focuses on medical ethics, post-pandemic policy review, hospital visitor restrictions, consumer protection, administrative accountability, and the limits of regulation.

About

Research begins where institutions stop explaining themselves.

George Kujo is an independent researcher and public commentator working at the intersection of medical ethics, public policy, consumer protection, and institutional accountability.

His work examines how emergency measures become permanent systems, how administrative decisions affect individual rights, and how evidence can be translated into accountable public policy.

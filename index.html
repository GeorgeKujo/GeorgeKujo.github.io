About

Research begins where institutions stop explaining themselves.

George Kujo is an independent researcher and public commentator working across medical ethics, public policy, consumer protection, and institutional accountability.

His work uses public records, secondary data, citation audit, and adversarial review to examine the distance between formal rights and real-world remedies.
